About Nutrishield

Nutrishield helps people quickly determine if a food item contains allergens and learn its basic nutrition so they can choose meals with confidence.

Problem we solve

Hidden allergens and unclear labels make eating risky for many. Nutrishield reduces uncertainty by highlighting potential allergens and summarizing nutrition clearly.

Who it's for

Designed for anyone managing allergies, families preparing meals, students, and fitness-minded users who want quick, reliable food information.

Simple — Fast — Reliable

How it helps

Nutrishield checks ingredient lists against a user’s allergy profile and presents an easy-to-read nutrition summary so unsafe items are flagged and healthier options are visible.

  • Create an allergy profile — list ingredients you must avoid.
  • Search foods — get ingredient breakdown and nutrition data.
  • View history — revisit previous checks and saved items.
Purpose
Identify hidden allergens and present clear nutrition information for safer food choices.
How it works
Compare listed ingredients to your allergy profile and summarize calories, protein, fats, and carbs.
What you can do
Add allergies, search or scan foods, view warnings, and save items for later reference.
Benefits
Reduces risk, speeds decisions, and helps users learn about the nutrition of everyday foods.
✓ Quick synopsis

Nutrishield is a user-focused tool that makes it simple to check foods for allergens and see the nutrition summary at a glance — practical for daily use and meal planning.
